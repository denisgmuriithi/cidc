Changing Diabetes® in Children

OUR PARTNERS

The Changing Diabetes® in Children in Kenya (CDiC) program is an international program for children with type 1 diabetes launched by Novo Nordisk Education Foundation (NNEF) and is part of Novo Nordisk’s Access to Diabetes Care strategy and builds on the UN-defined cornerstones in the right to health.


Due to the complex nature of diabetes in children, to address this challenge, a coordinated approach that targets all barriers to care is required. The Changing Diabetes(r) in Children programme has been implemented in collaboration with both international and local partners which include: Novo Nordisk, World Diabetes Foundation (WDF),Roche Diabetes, Kenya DMI and Pediatric Endocrine Society Kenya.
