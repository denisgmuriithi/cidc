Changing Diabetes® in Children

CDIC- Vision

The aim of Changing Diabetes in Children (CDiC) program in Kenya is to reduce child mortality, by breaking down the barriers that prevent children with type 1 diabetes in developing countries from receiving proper care, through the following:

Treatment

Care, compassion and commitment for children with diabetes through changing ® initiatives.

Capacity Building

Early diagnosis and management to prevent or delay serious complications.

Awareness/ Advocacy

Enhancing Healthcare by facilitating awareness and education/empower stakeholders with knowledge about the state of diabetes care.
